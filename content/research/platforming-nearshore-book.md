## The Industry's First Book on the Science of Nearshore Talent

**"Platforming Nearshore: The Science of High-Performance LATAM Talent"** is the definitive guide for technology leaders looking to build and scale elite engineering teams in Latin America. This book provides a rigorous, data-driven framework for everything from talent evaluation and cultural integration to performance management and long-term retention.

### What You'll Learn:

*   **The Science of Talent Evaluation:** Go beyond resumes and traditional interviews with a proprietary framework for identifying top-tier LATAM engineers.
*   **Building a High-Trust Culture:** Learn the keys to integrating nearshore teams for maximum collaboration and psychological safety.
*   **Data-Driven Performance Management:** Implement a system for measuring and improving the performance of your nearshore engineers.
*   **Long-Term Retention Strategies:** Discover the secrets to retaining top LATAM talent for the long haul.
